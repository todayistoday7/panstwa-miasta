How to play

1

Create a room, share the code — 3 to 10 players

2

Each round one player is the Psychic — they see where the target is on the spectrum

3

The Psychic gives a ONE-WORD clue that hints at the position

4

Everyone else moves the dial to their best guess

5

Bullseye = 4pts · Close = 3pts · Near = 2pts · Outer = 1pt
