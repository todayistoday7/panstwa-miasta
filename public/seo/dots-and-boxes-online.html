Dots and Boxes Online

Dots and Boxes is a classic strategy game — now playable online with friends, family or your team, for free, on any device. Create a room, share the code and start playing in under a minute.

How to Play Dots and Boxes Online

Players take turns drawing one horizontal or vertical line between two adjacent dots. When a player completes the fourth side of a box, they claim it, mark it with their initial, and get another turn. The game ends when all boxes are claimed — the player with the most boxes wins.

Grid Sizes

  • 3×3 — 9 boxes, quick game (~5 minutes)
  • 4×4 — 16 boxes, standard game (~10 minutes)
  • 5×5 — 25 boxes, longer game (~15 minutes)
  • 6×6 — 36 boxes, for experienced players (~25 minutes)
