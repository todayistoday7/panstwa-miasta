Dots and Boxes Online

Dots and Boxes is a classic strategy game — now playable online with friends, family or your team, for free, on any device. Create a room, share the code and start playing in under a minute.

Take turns drawing lines between dots on the grid. Complete the fourth side of a box to claim it and take another turn. The player with the most boxes at the end wins. Simple enough for kids from age 5, strategic enough to keep adults hooked.

It works just as well for a family game night as it does for a remote team ice breaker. Because there are no words — just lines and dots — it's a perfect fit for international and multicultural teams. No language barrier, no complicated rules to explain. A quick game takes 5-10 minutes, making it ideal for a coffee break during a video call or a warm-up before a workshop.

Works on desktop, tablet and mobile. No download, no signup — just share a link.

How to Play Dots and Boxes Online

Players take turns drawing one horizontal or vertical line between two adjacent dots. When a player completes the fourth side of a box, they claim it, mark it with their initial, and get another turn. The game ends when all boxes are claimed — the player with the most boxes wins.

Grid Sizes

  • 3×3 — 9 boxes, quick game (~5 minutes)
  • 4×4 — 16 boxes, standard game (~10 minutes)
  • 5×5 — 25 boxes, longer game (~15 minutes)
  • 6×6 — 36 boxes, for experienced players (~25 minutes)
