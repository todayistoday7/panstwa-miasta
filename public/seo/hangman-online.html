Hangman Online

Hangman is the classic word guessing game — now playable online with friends, family or your team, for free, on any device. No signup, no download.

One player picks a secret word, everyone else guesses letters. Each wrong guess adds a part to the gallows. 7 wrong guesses and it's game over — can you solve the word in time? Works for 2-10 players.

A brilliant educational and ice breaker game for kids from age 8 and adults alike. Perfect for online classes, team meetings, family game nights or playing with friends across different cities.

🪢 2-10 players👶 Ages 8+🆓 Free to play📱 Mobile friendly📚 Educational

How to Play Hangman Online

The word picker types a secret word. Everyone else guesses letters by clicking the keyboard. Each correct letter is revealed in the word. Each wrong letter adds a body part to the gallows. 7 wrong guesses ends the round.

Who Is This Game For?

Hangman online is perfect for kids learning new vocabulary, adults who enjoy word games, team building sessions and anyone who wants a quick fun game that works on any device without any setup.
