Sketch and Guess Online

Sketch and Guess is a drawing chain game where words pass through a series of drawings and guesses — getting hilariously distorted along the way. Free, for 4-10 players, on any device.

Draw a word, guess a drawing — and watch how your original phrase transforms beyond recognition over several rounds. No points, no competition — pure fun and laughs for everyone.

Perfect as a remote team ice breaker — no language barrier since everyone draws. Great for parties, team events and as a warm-up before workshops. Bad drawings make it even funnier!

🎨 4-10 players👶 Ages 8+🆓 Free to play📱 Mobile friendly🎉 No points

How to Play Sketch and Guess?

Each player writes a secret word. Words rotate around the group — the next person draws it, the next guesses what it shows, the next draws their guess and so on. At the end everyone sees the full chain of how their word evolved. The worse the drawing, the funnier the result!

Who Is This Game For?

Sketch and Guess is perfect for groups who want fun without competition. Great for parties, team building, video calls and as a warm-up activity before workshops or meetings.
