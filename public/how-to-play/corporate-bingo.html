How to Play Corporate Bingo Online

Corporate Bingo is the game for anyone who has sat through one too many meetings full of buzzwords. Everyone gets a unique card — and you quietly tick off phrases as they happen in real time. Free, for 2-50 players, works on any device.

How It Works

The host creates a room and shares the code. Everyone joins on their phone or laptop and gets their own 5×5 card with corporate phrases — each card is unique, so not everyone has the same squares.

During the meeting you play normally. When you hear a phrase on your card, tap it. Get five in a row — horizontal, vertical or diagonal — and you win.

Example Phrases

Cards include classics like "synergy", "circle back", "deep dive", "bandwidth", "take it offline" and "move the needle". Sound familiar?

Scoring

When you complete a line, tap BINGO — the game confirms it and all players see who won. You can play competitively (first to BINGO wins) or just for fun as a shared running joke.

💡 Tip: Works brilliantly on Zoom or Teams. Keep it open in a tab while your camera is on — your colleagues will never know what you are really up to.

Settings

  • 2-50 players — scales to any team size
  • Private or public room — invite your team with a code or join a public room
  • No signup needed — just a name and the room code
