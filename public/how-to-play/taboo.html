How to Play Taboo Online?

Taboo is a team party game where you describe words without using the forbidden expressions. Available online, free, for 4-12 players.

Teams and Roles

Players are randomly split into two teams: red and blue. Each round one team describes words while the other watches and enforces rules through their referee.

The Describer

One player from the active team sees the secret word and its list of forbidden words. They must describe it to their team without using any forbidden word.

The Referee

One player from the opposing team acts as referee. If the describer uses a forbidden word, the referee can award a penalty point to the opposing team.

Scoring

  • +1 point for the team — for each correctly guessed word
  • +1 point for the opposing team — when the referee catches a forbidden word

Round Timer

Each describing turn lasts 60 seconds by default. The host can change this before the game. When time runs out, roles switch to the other team.

How to Win

The team with more points after all rounds wins. The number of rounds is configurable by the host.

💡 Tip: Use context and purpose — instead of saying what the thing is, describe what it's used for or when you'd encounter it.
