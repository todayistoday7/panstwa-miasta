How to Play Find Pairs Online

Find Pairs is a classic card matching game — now free to play online with friends and family. Take turns flipping two cards to find matching emoji pairs. Simple enough for kids from age 4, but genuinely fun for all ages.

What You Need

  • 2–6 players — perfect for families and small groups
  • Any device — phone, tablet or laptop
  • No download, no signup — just share a room code
  • Ages 4+ — simple rules everyone can learn in seconds

Game Setup

One player creates a room and shares the 5-letter code. Before starting, the host chooses:

  • Theme — Animals 🐶🐱🦁, Food 🍕🍦🍎, Nature 🌸🌊🌈, Travel ✈️🚀🏰, Sports ⚽🏀🎾, or Flags 🇵🇱🇬🇧🇩🇪
  • Board size — 3×4 (12 cards, easiest), 4×4 (16 cards), or 4×5 (20 cards, hardest)
  • Max players — 2 to 6
  • Room visibility — Private (code only) or Public (visible on the Live Rooms page)

💡 Playing with young kids? Use the 3×4 board with the Animals theme — 6 pairs, easy to manage, and kids love the animal emojis.

How to Play

Players take turns flipping two cards at a time:

  • Tap any card to flip it face-up and reveal the emoji
  • Tap a second card — if it matches the first, you've found a pair!
  • Match? You keep the pair and get another turn immediately
  • No match? Both cards flip back face-down. Try to remember where they were!
  • Next player's turn — play continues clockwise

The key skill is remembering which cards you've already seen. When someone flips a card you remember seeing before, you'll know where its match is on your next turn.

Scoring & Winning

Each matched pair scores 1 point. The game ends when all pairs have been found. The player with the most pairs wins. In case of a tie, multiple winners are shown.

💡 Strategy tip: On your first few turns, flip cards in different areas of the board to see as many different emojis as possible. This builds your mental map for later turns.

Themes & Board Sizes

The 6 emoji themes keep the game fresh across multiple rounds. Each theme has its own set of emojis that are shuffled randomly. The 3 board sizes offer different challenge levels:

  • 3×4 (12 cards, 6 pairs) — quick rounds, great for young children
  • 4×4 (16 cards, 8 pairs) — the standard experience, balanced for all ages
  • 4×5 (20 cards, 10 pairs) — longer rounds, more challenging recall

Play Again

After each game, the host can start a rematch with the same group. The theme and board size can be changed between rounds. Try a different theme each time to keep it interesting!

Frequently Asked Questions

What age is it suitable for?

From age 4. The rules are very simple and young children often outperform adults thanks to their sharp visual recall.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes — the game works on any device with a browser. Phone, tablet, laptop. No app needed.

What if someone disconnects?

If a player accidentally leaves, they can rejoin with the same name and room code. Their score and position are preserved.

How long does a game take?

A 3×4 game takes about 2-3 minutes. A 4×5 game can take 5-8 minutes depending on how many players there are.
