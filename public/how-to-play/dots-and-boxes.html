How to Play Dots & Boxes Online?

Dots and Boxes is a classic strategy game for 2-4 players. Draw lines between dots and claim boxes — the player with the most boxes wins.

Rules

Take turns drawing a line

Players take turns drawing one line between two adjacent dots (horizontal or vertical). Click a line to draw it.

Complete a box = point + extra turn

If your line completes the fourth side of a square — you score a point and play again! You can score multiple points in a row.

The game ends when...

All lines are drawn and all boxes are claimed. The player with the most boxes wins.

Strategy

💡 Tip: Avoid drawing the third line on an uncompleted box — you hand the opponent an easy point. Try to create long chains of boxes for yourself to claim.

Settings

  • Grid size — 3×3 (9 boxes), 4×4 (16), 5×5 (25) or 6×6 (36)
  • Players — 2 to 4

A bigger grid means a longer, more strategic game. For a quick round, choose 3×3.
