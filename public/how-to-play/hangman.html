How to Play Hangman Online?

Hangman is the classic word guessing game — each round one player thinks of a secret word while everyone else tries to guess it letter by letter. Available online for 2-10 players.

Player Rotation

Each round one player is the Word Picker — they type the secret word. All other players are Guessers. Roles rotate — everyone takes a turn being the Picker.

How a Round Works

1. Picker enters a word

The Picker sees a word input field and an optional hint field. They can also tap "Random Word" to get a suggestion from the word bank.

2. Guessers guess letters

Guessers see dashes (one per letter) and an alphabet keyboard. Click letters to guess. A correct letter reveals itself in the word.

3. Wrong guesses

Each wrong letter adds a part to the hangman: head, body, left arm, right arm, left leg, right leg, face. 7 wrong guesses = game over, Picker wins the round.

4. Guess the full word

At any time you can try to guess the entire word. Correct — you win the round. Wrong — instant loss!

Scoring

  • +1 point for the Guesser — whoever guesses the word first
  • +1 point for the Picker — if nobody guesses (hangman complete)

Winning

After every player has been the Picker once, the game ends. The player with the most points wins.

💡 Picker tip: Pick words with rare letters — guessers will burn through their alphabet trying to find them. A good category hint makes the game more fun without making it too easy.
