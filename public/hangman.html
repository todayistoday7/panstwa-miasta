How to play

1

Create a room — 2 to 10 players

2

Each round one player picks a secret word — everyone else guesses

3

Tap letters to guess — 7 wrong guesses and the hangman is complete!

4

You can also guess the full word — but wrong = instant loss!

5

Everyone takes a turn picking a word. Most wins at the end takes the game.
